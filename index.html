About Us

Home Care Service Agency was established in 2010 as a professional agency dedicated to providing high-quality in-home care services. We specialize in offering professional, reliable, and compassionate services to clients who need long-term care, short-term rehabilitation, or daily assistance.

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our clients through personalized, professional care services, allowing them to maintain dignity and independence in the familiar and comfortable environment of their own home. We believe that everyone deserves the best care possible, regardless of their age or health condition.

Our team consists of experienced, professionally trained caregivers who not only possess the necessary skills but also demonstrate compassion and patience to provide comprehensive care to our clients.

Compassion

We treat every client with love and patience, providing warm, human-centered care.

Professionalism

All our caregivers are professionally trained and carefully screened to ensure quality care.

Reliability

We provide around-the-clock service, responding to client needs promptly, giving families peace of mind.

Our Services

Professional Nursing Care

Includes wound care, medication management, vital signs monitoring, and other professional medical nursing services provided by registered nurses.

Personal Care Assistance

Assisting clients with daily personal hygiene care, including bathing, dressing, and toileting, while maintaining client dignity.

Meal Preparation & Nutrition

Preparing nutritious meals based on client health conditions and dietary preferences to ensure healthy eating.

Household Assistance

Providing light housekeeping services such as cleaning, laundry, and shopping to maintain a clean and comfortable home environment.

Companionship & Activities

Providing companionship services, participating in social activities, and engaging in appropriate physical exercise to promote physical and mental health.

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Providing professional care for clients recovering from surgery to accelerate the recovery process and improve quality of life.
